End Of Cold War

the USSR began to crumble as liberal reforms proved difficult to handle and capitalist changes to the centralized economy were badly transitioned and caused major problems. After a series of revolutions in Soviet Bloc states and a failed coup by conservative elements opposed to the ongoing reforms. the Soviet Union collapsed in December 25 1991 and the Cold War came to an end.
