Fall of the berlin wall

The fall of the Berlin Wall (German: Mauerfall) began the evening of 9 November 1989 and continued over the following days and weeks, with people nicknamed Mauerspechte using various tools to chip off souvenirs demolishing lengthy parts in the process and creating several unofficial border crossings.
